This domain looks at the commercial relationships between travel creators, brands, tourism boards, hotels, agencies, destinations, and platforms.

Coverage includes campaign structure, creator fit, deliverables, usage rights, rates, reporting, disclosure, negotiation, and the difference between short-term exposure and long-term partnership value.

The focus is on partnerships that are clear, ethical, measurable, and sustainable.
