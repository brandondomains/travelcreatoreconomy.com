This domain tracks how travel creators navigate platforms that control discovery, monetization, and audience access.

Coverage includes algorithm changes, search volatility, social reach, newsletter platforms, video monetization, creator tools, and the risks of building too heavily on rented infrastructure.

The goal is to help creators think about platforms as channels, not foundations.
