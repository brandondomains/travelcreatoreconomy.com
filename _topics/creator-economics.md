This domain examines the larger economic forces shaping travel blogging and creator-led travel media.

Coverage includes pricing power, creator labor, market saturation, demand from brands and destinations, professionalization, platform incentives, and the economics of attention in travel publishing.

The focus is on understanding the industry as a market, not just a set of content channels.
