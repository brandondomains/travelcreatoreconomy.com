This domain examines how travel creators earn money and how different revenue streams affect editorial strategy, audience trust, and long-term stability.

Coverage includes affiliate programs, display advertising, direct sponsorships, paid products, consulting, memberships, licensing, and hybrid models that combine several income streams.

The goal is to understand not only what earns revenue, but what creates resilient economics for independent travel media.
